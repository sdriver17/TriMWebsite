Criteria For Admission to Tri-M Chapter 3229

A student who wishes to become a member of Tri-M must:

  • Perform a NYSSMA (New York State School Music Association) SOLO on his or her instrument attaining the following guideline scores:

    • Level IV (4) a minimum score of '21'

    • Level V (5) a minimum score of '90'

    • Level VI (6) a minimum score of '90'

  • Achieve a score of '3', '4' or '5' on the Advanced Placement Examination in Music Theory prior to the fall induction, as an option for students who take Music Theory.

  • Write an "Essay of Intent" to the TRI-M Advisor using the following:

    • 100 -125 words

    • Double-spaced type, Times New Roman 12-font and 1" margins

    • A response to the Essential Question: "What makes me worthy of this distinction, and how will I contribute to the Tri-M Music Honor Society?"

  • Ensemble or Music Theory grade requirement of "93" and overall GPA (grade point average) of "85".

  • Four teacher evaluation forms from other non music teachers from the previous year.

  • If you want a quick list to check off your progress go to the application checklist page!
