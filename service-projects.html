Tri-M Service Projects

If you would like to do a project that is not listed below, please discuss the project with the Tri-M advisor before completing it.

  • Usher at concerts and performance events (Middle and High School)

  • Create catalogue and electronic filing system for band, choral and/or orchestral libraries (A-G, H-M, N-R, S-Z) by title or composer (ask the director)

  • Assist ensemble director with other needs

  • Perform for Briarcliff HS Tri-M sanctioned events at outside venues such as, but not limited to:

    • Assisted living centers

    • Veterans homes

    • School open houses

    • Hospitals

    • PTA and/or approved District events/meetings

    • Community functions

  • Find guest speakers for meetings (local performers/composers)

  • Sing/play the National Anthem at school or local events

  • Research and report to our chapter (#3229) on state and national activities in Tri-M

  • Serve as set-up crew for ensemble concerts/trips/special events etc. (trip helpers)

  • Perform for the Holiday Caroling Performance

  • Rehearse and perform with Pit Orchestra (serves as two projects)

  • Tutor/teach elementary/middle/high school music students on a regular weekly basis throughout the school year (serves as three projects)
