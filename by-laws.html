Briarcliff Chapter 3229 By-Laws

To remain a member in good standing, the Tri-M member must follow these guidelines:

Music Participation

  • Attend all performances of his/her ensemble within and outside of the school setting (barring documented family emergencies and/or prior written notification that are deemed "acceptable" to the ensemble director)

  • Perform a NYSSMA Solo at the Spring Solo Festival and achieve the requisite score found in the Criteria for Admission

  • Attend all ensemble rehearsals and sectionals regularly and on-time

  • Be prepared for all ensemble rehearsals and lessons

  • Every Tri-M member is expected to perform in some or all of these extra-curricular functions in the Briarcliff School District community

    • Selected District Sports Events

    • Kids Care

    • Graduation

    • Special Assemblies

    • Tri-M Meetings

    • Pit Orchestra

    • Briarcliff Rotary Club

Grades/Scholarship

  • Maintain a grade point average of 93% in ensemble class (Band, Orchestra, Chorus or Music Theory Class)

  • Maintain a cumulative grade point average of 85%

  • *Achieve a score of "3", "4" or "5" on AP Music Theory Exam

Character

  • Adhere to the BHS CODE OF CONDUCT at all times

  • Be respectful towards teachers, staff and peers

  • Exhibit respectful and courteous behavior to his/her ensemble director at all times

Service

  • Regularly attend chapter meetings

  • Participate in Tri-M Induction Ceremony and corresponding rehearsals

  • Complete three (3) projects that are listed in section V. By:

    • Friday, May 5, 2017, for Seniors and Friday, June 9, 2017, for all other students (Seniors must fulfill their Tri-M service requirement in order to receive the Tri-M cord at the graduation). Each project should take approximately 1-2 hours to complete.

    • Seniors who have a perfect attendance record for all meetings throughout the year will earn a medallion that can be worn at the graduation.

  • Sufficient attendance at chapter meetings per year

  • Pay $20.00 membership fee (annually)

Punishment

The Tri-M chapter meetings will be held on the 1st and 3rd Wednesdays of each month. On occasion additional meetings might be held with ample notice.

Missing 3 meetings will result in the issuance of a warning. Missing 4 meetings will result in the member being put on probation. Missing 5 meetings will result in the dismissal of the member from the chapter.

* Tri-M members admitted under the AP Exam Score of 3/4/5 performance criteria are exempt from the NYSSMA solo requirement. However, these students are strongly urged to perform with an in-school or after-school ensemble.

NON-SELECTION: TRI-M MEMBERSHIP IS AN HONOR BESTOWED UPON A STUDENT AS A PRIVILEGE, NOT A RIGHT. SPECIAL EFFORTS WILL BE MADE TO EXPLAIN THE SELECTION PROCESS TO THOSE MUSIC STUDENTS WHO ARE ACDEMICALLY ELIGIBLE YET NOT SELECTED FOR CANDIDACY

SELECTION REVIEW: TRI-M AND NAFME SHALL NOT REVIEW THE JUDGEMENT OF THE CHAPTER ADVISOR OR ANY COMMITTEE INVOLVED REGARDING SELECTION OF INDIVIDUAL MEMBERS
